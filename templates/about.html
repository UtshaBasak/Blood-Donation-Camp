About Our Blood Donation Service

Welcome to our Blood Donation Service. We connect donors and receivers to ensure a seamless blood donation process.

Donors

Donors provide the life-saving blood. They register with their personal details and blood type.

Receivers

Receivers are individuals in need of blood. They can request blood based on their requirements.

Blood Banks

Blood banks store and manage blood donations. They ensure the availability and quality of blood.

Blood Requests

Blood requests are made by receivers. These requests are fulfilled by matching available blood from donors or blood banks.
